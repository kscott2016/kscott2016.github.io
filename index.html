Scar's Bio
Scar is the main antagonist of Disney's 1994 animated film, The Lion King. He is the younger brother of Mufasa and uncle of Simba. He coveted his brother's position as King of the Pridelands. With his hyena henchmen, he killed Mufasa and attempted to kill Simba.
Scar's Hyenas
Scar controls a group of hyenas to overthrow the Pridelands. The main 3 are Bonzai, Shenzi, and Ed.

Cruella's Bio
Cruella DeVil is the main antagonist from the 1961 animated movie, 101 Dalmations. She loves fashion and riches, but nothing tops her obcession with furs. She along with her two henchmen, Jasper and Horace, collect 99 dalmation puppies to make a fur coat.
Cruella's Henchmen
Cruella hires 2 petty criminals Jasper and Horace to help her steal the puppies. Jasper considers himself the smarter one, but he actually falls for more traps than Horace.

Maleficient's Bio
Maleficient is an evil fairy and main antagonist from the 1959 animated movie, Sleeping Beauty.She is offened from not being invited to Princess Aurora's christening. She cursed Aurora, to prick her finger on a spinning wheel on her 16th birthday. She is an incarnation of pure evil.

Her goons are a group of gargoyle like creatures. They seem to be extremely stupid. For the 16 years of Aurora's disappearance, they were searching for a baby.

Jafar's Bio
