Welcome to my site! I’m a junior majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Motion Pictures and Art at the University of Miami. I always had an interest in creating graphic effects and creating amazing stories. Before I enrolled at the University of Miami, I did not have video editing classes available to me. I was determined to learn more about video editing and graphic design so I taught myself. I’m proud to say that I’m a self taught video editor! I have experience in iMovie, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere, and Avid. I created many recreational videos, videos for classes, videos for my church, and high school.

At the University of Miami, I've enrolled in Digital Production classes, Screenwriting classes, Art classes, and Programming classes. Outside of the classroom, I design promootional material for Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority Inc. and the University of Miami's chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers. If you ask any of my friends, they will tell you that I'm a huge fan of anything animated! I love animations and graphics, because they allow you to look at things in a completely different way and they're fun! They allow others to immerse themselves in a world of vivid imagery and creativity.

Besides graphics and animations, I'm passionate about mentoring youth. Children are our future. If we want any positive change in our society, we have to educate children the best way we can. In the future, I plan to develop a game that will introduce children to try a spectrum of different careers. I believe the reason why there is disparity for minority representation in the STEM (Science,Engineering, Technology, and Mathematics) fields, because they are only introduced to the difficulty of those fields and don't know how to properly prepare for those careers. The more things you expose yourself to, the more you're willing to explore the field. Plus I've believe to effectively teach children things, it needs to be presented in a fun way. With the completion of this project, I hope youth will not be afraid to try new things and believe any dream they have, they can achieve it.

Please feel free to look at my work and check out my resume. Thanks for visiting!
